Super Resellers

Creating a mobile app for resellers

Client

Super Resellers

Location

Brazil

Year

2018 - 2020

Super Resellers is an app for people who resell products from authorized companies. With it, users can have better management of everything related to their work.

It is possible to manage clients, sales, inventory, and much more.

Additionally, each user of the app gets a website upon creating an account, which displays all their products for sale.
